Speaker3304: Tinianan also has 4 vowel marks, the acute accent (á) marks the stressed vowel following some rules that aren't important now, the grave acute (à) makes different sounds depending on the vowel, ò makes a less-deep u sound, à makes an ow sound like in the word "saw", ù makes an ü sound (again text-to-speech is unable to recreate it) and è makes an ea sound like in the word "pearl".
